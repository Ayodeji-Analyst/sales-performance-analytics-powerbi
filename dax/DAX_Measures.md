# 📐 DAX Measures

This document contains the DAX measures used in the Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard.

---

## 💰 Total Sales

```DAX
Total Sales = SUM(Sales[Sales])
```

---

## 💵 Total Profit

```DAX
Total Profit = SUM(Sales[Profit])
```

---

## 📦 Total Quantity

```DAX
Total Quantity = SUM(Sales[Quantity])
```
